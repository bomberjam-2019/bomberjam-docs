## Point System


## Dying

When a player dies, they respawn to there starting point. This is one of the corners.
That player is than invincible for a period of time. (See [`Player`](/api/#Player.respawning)). During that time, the player wil be able to move, but not drop bombs.

When a player dies, they will not lose their bonuses. 

## Sudden Death

After 1000 ticks, the game will enter sudden death. When a player dies during sudden death, they will NOT respawn.
